Katie Brethorst

About Me

Katie is a writer who spends her days attending the University of Illinois at Springfield, hoping that her student loans will earn her a Bachelor's degree in history, and not just nightmares of the IRS bursting into her house. Besides writing, she has worked at a movie theater, where the popcorn riddled aisles have inspired her hellscapes, and the customers have shown her how to create the vilest of villains. Seeing as Katie is a history fanatic, she has spent her summers as a tour guide at the Dana-Thomas House, which is a Frank Lloyd Wright house, not that she likes to highlight him because he was a terrible person. Instead on her tours she loves to lecture on about Susan Lawrence Dana, the under appreciated owner of the home who saved lives, was an overall badass, and who is constantly overshadowed by Springfield's god, Abraham Lincoln. Katie loves playing with her rescue pit bull Rhett and looks for him for emotional support when she kills off yet another character or can't think of anything witty to write. Katie also has a passion for photography, reading, and procrastinating her homework assignments. Should you for any reason think this person sounds interesting, please feel free to contact her at phanofpanic14@gmail.com.
